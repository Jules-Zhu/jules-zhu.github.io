Bienvenue

Coucou les amis ! Bienvenue à mon Site Web Personnel ! Moi, c’est Jules. J’étudie l’algèbre, par exemple, Algèbre Amassée, Algèbre de Lie, Théorie Algébrique des Nombres et Algèbre Géométrique. Je m’intéresse aussi à l’analyse et à la géométrie. Je peux parler chinois et un peu anglais. J’apprends aussi le français en ce moment. C’est mon professeur qui m’a introduit à l’algèbre: Huang Min.

Hello everyone, my name is ZHU Haoyu, and I am currently a third-year undergraduate student majoring in mathematics. My journey in algebra began in my sophomore year with an introduction to group theory, ring theory, and a bit of field theory. In the following semester, I studied commutative algebra and representation theory, including Lie algebras and an introduction to Coxeter groups. More recently, I explored cluster algebras, their connections to quiver representations and cluster categories, and the theory of quantum cluster algebras, under the instruction of professor Huang Min.

I am eager to engage with cutting-edge research. Last summer, I collaborated with researchers at Peking University on Bott–Samelson rings for Coxeter groups. We provided explicit presentations of these rings using generators and relations and discovered interesting combinatorial properties. Currently, I am studying the correspondence between skein algebras and quantum cluster algebras of , aiming to extend these results to stated skein algebras and other semisimple Lie algebras.

I enjoy learning mathematics with others. Hosting and participating in seminars allows me to exchange ideas with peers, as I believe discussion and collaboration are keys to deepening our understanding and gaining new insights.

Thank you!
